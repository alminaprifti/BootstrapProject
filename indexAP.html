Tax ID Application

An ITIN (Individual Taxpayer Identification Number, type of Tax ID or Taxpayer ID) will serve as your identification number for filing your tax returns. If you do not qualify for an SSN, you will need to apply for an ITIN

Your ITIN tax ID not only helps you file a tax return, it ensures you get the refund you deserve as quickly as possible
